THE LAUGH SQUARE

They say laughter is the best medicine. I'm not really sure who "they" are or what sickness laughter is best for but who am I to question them? What I do know is that laughter is contagious (It might be a virus) but it's a lot more fun than the flu.

Warning: Laughter is not intended to diagnose, treat or cure any medical conditions. Some people have experienced changes in behavior, flushing, shortness of breath, light-headedness, numbness, watering eyes and runny nose while laughing. If you experience these or any other abnormal symptoms, stop laughing and call your doctor immediately.
